TeeTime

One App. One Vision. A Future for Families Everywhere.

Welcome

Hi, I'm Anthony Thompson — a father, a husband, and someone who understands what it’s like to rebuild life from nothing. I’m building TeeTime: a powerful all-in-one app where people can send, receive, and store money like Cash App or PayPal — and access information like Google. It’s a mission of love, legacy, and purpose.

I’ve lost a lot: my brother, my freedom, and my mother to Alzheimer’s and dementia. But I never lost my will to make a better future for my wife and kids — and for you. TeeTime is my way of doing that.

Why TeeTime?

  • Send & receive money instantly — safer, smarter, and rewarding.
  • Search the web seamlessly — get answers with no ads or tracking.
  • Earn rewards just for using it daily.
  • Support mental health and family stability with every download.
